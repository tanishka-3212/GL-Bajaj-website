ABOUT US:

GL Bajaj Institute of Technology and Management is the 6th Institute established under the prestigious banner of Rajeev Memorial Academic Welfare Society. The institute is approved by AICTE and affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (Formerly UPTU Lucknow).

GL Bajaj Institute of Technology and Management is a quality-driven educational institution in the Greater Noida/Delhi-NCR region, offering programs in B.Tech, MBA, and MCA. It stands as one of the best engineering and management colleges in UP, ranked among the top institutes by NIRF, and has maintained the highest pass percentage in AKTU university results.
